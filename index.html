About

My name is Shuntoria Reid. I am a college student studying Health and Human Services, a Code the Dream Intro to Programming student, a bug bounty beginner, and the owner of a plus size fashion brand, Major Pressure Plustique. My journey includes overcoming homelessness, addiction, and justice system involvement, and turning that pain into purpose.

I am passionate about building tools and experiences that uplift people especially women, plus size communities, and folks who grew up feeling overlooked. In tech, I love seeing my code come to life in the browser, connecting APIs, and using data to drive experiences. In fashion and business, I focus on confidence, representation, and community impact.
